## Notes

- Needs a USB3.0 port.
- Needs an HDMI signal to show up as an audio device.

## Power Usage

- 1080p30 NV12 capture: x watts.
- 4k30 NV12 capture: y watts.
- 1080p30 YUYV capture: z watts.
