# Macrosilicon MS2130 (4k)

An updated/sister device to the [Macrosilicon MS2109](ms2109.md) capture devices. Offers uncompressed video up to 1080p60, but accepts input up to 4k30 and scales internally. Hardware quality seems about what one would expect.

May be called the Mini U3 or similar.

## Notes

- Seems better than the MS2109 cards it replaces.
- Chroma is strongly compressed, which lowers image quality.
- On a USB2 port, YUYV output is capped at 1080p10, MJPEG output is capped at 1080p50 (this may depend on firmware?).
- Not all firmwares supports MJPEG.
- Some firmwares seem to have a bug where the very bottom row of pixels is all black.
- If you feel like fiddling with the firmware, the [ms-tools](https://github.com/BertoldVdb/ms-tools) GitHub repo is a good start.

## Power Usage

- 1080p30 MJPEG capture: 1.5 watts.
- 1080p60 MJPEG capture: 1.85 watts.
- 1080p30 YUYV capture: 0.75 watts.
- 1080p60 YUYV capture: 0.8 watts.
